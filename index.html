About

üçÖgraph (or Tomatograph) is the annual Ontario-Quebec pre-SIGGRAPH workshop, organized by Central-Canadian computer graphics labs. It is an opportunity for students to present their projects to a friendly, constructively critical audience and to get valuable feedback.

Program

üçÖgraph will be held distributed among various rooms in the Bahen Centre for Information Technology. Registrants will receive a link to a spreadsheet containing their rotating room assignments.

Short talks will each be 10-12mins + 3-5mins of Q&A.

Talks, keynotes, panels, and best practice presentations will be broadcast from BA 5166 to all other rooms via Zoom.

Small group discussions are open format. Each group is encouraged to start with introductions and begin with someone who hasn't yet talked about their own work in a previous group. Bring your laptop and show demos, results or other visual aides.
